Welcome to the exciting world of my 3D CV application! I am Biolan Lucian, the developer and creator of this unique experience. My CV app is not just a simple digital portfolio but also an interactive game that allows you to explore and interact with my portfolio elements in a fun and engaging way.

Get Started

Start the game: To begin, press the 'Start' button

Camera control: Use the left mouse button to rotate the camera around the characters and objects.
With the right mouse button, you can move the camera left or right to view details from different angles.

Zoom in and Zoom out: Use the mouse scroll wheel to zoom in or zoom out for a more detailed exploration.

Move through the 3D scene and search for the hidden geometric shapes.

PS - Don't forget to turn up the volume!
